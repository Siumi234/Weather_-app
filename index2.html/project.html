SRI LANKA

The wonder of Asia

This small magnificent island in South Asia is definitely a mother nature's favourite. Throughout the year, Sri lanka, aka Ceylon attracts all kinds of tourists. The island having a tropical climate, makes it even better for the people who seeks its beauty.
Sri Lanka is home for hundreds of wild lives, especially for the Asian elephant; for both wild elephants, and the rescued ones at Pinnawala elephant orphanage where you can see and feed this enormous creatures.
In addition to that, you can enjoy the cold weather and the tea plantations of central hills, the warm colorfulness of Jaffna,the mesmerizing city life of Colombo,the capitals and many more.
And to wrap it all up, Sri Lanka is one of the 'at-least-once-destinations', of every traveller's life.
